Directive Principles of State Policy

What are Directive Principles?

The Directive Principles of State Policy are guidelines that help the government make decisions for the good of everyone. They make sure everyone has what they need to live a good life, like education, health, and equality.

Key Directive Principles

  • 1. Free and compulsory education for children.
  • 2. Equal pay for equal work for both men and women.
  • 3. Protecting the environment and safeguarding forests.
  • 4. Promoting health and nutrition for everyone.
  • 5. Providing help to people who are in need.
  • 6. Making sure the country's wealth is shared fairly.
  • 7. Promoting international peace and security.
